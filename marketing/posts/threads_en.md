Small thing I made: an iOS app that opens directly to the nearest free public toilet in Seoul. Distance, walking minutes, one tap to start directions. No account, no tracking, no map you have to pan around.

Seoul publishes open data for all ~4,400 free public toilets in the city — I just mapped them and made the home screen answer the question instead of asking you to search for it.

Free, iOS 17+, works in English, 中文, 日本語, 한국어.
